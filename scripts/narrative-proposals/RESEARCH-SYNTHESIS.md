# O Oraculo v3 — Narrative Research Synthesis

## Source Material Analysis
20 works from psychoanalysts (Desktop/Oraculo/) analyzed across 4 thematic clusters:

### Cluster 1: The Underground Self (Dostoevsky, Pessoa, Beckett, Clarice)
- Self-knowledge vs self-deception
- Identity as performance vs authentic existence
- The courage to face what disgusts
- 6 scenarios produced, best: The Thing on the Floor, The Crystal Room, The Voice That Will Not Stop

### Cluster 2: Desire, Memory & Loss (Freud x2, Proust, Sophocles)
- Transience and attachment
- Memory as creation vs prison
- Mourning as transformation
- 6 scenarios produced, best: The Garden That Will Burn, The Locked Room, The Two Waters

### Cluster 3: Power, Control & Freedom (Orwell, Huxley, Shakespeare x2, Milton)
- Inner surveillance (superego as Big Brother)
- Comfort vs truth
- The price of knowledge/consciousness
- 6 scenarios produced, best: The Comfortable Prison, The Fruit and the Garden, The Last Translation

### Cluster 4: Transformation & Wisdom (Hesse x2, Rilke, Drummond, Galeano)
- Patience vs urgency
- The ordinary as sacred
- Shared solitude
- 6 scenarios produced, best: The River, The Unanswerable, The Stone

## DEVOLUÇÃO Architecture — Pattern Reading

Instead of 64 unique devoluções (impossible), read across 2 axes:

### Axis 1: Movement vs Stillness
- Movement choices: stay in prison(A), hold still(B), enter garden(A), remember(A), carry question(A), ask for mirror(A)
- Stillness choices: look for door(B), pull away(A), turn away(B), forget(B), let dissolve(B), already know(B)

### Axis 2: Toward vs Away
- Toward: Moving into the uncomfortable, the unknown, the other
- Away: Protecting, preserving, maintaining distance

### 4 Dominant Patterns (devolução archetypes)

**1. The Seeker** (mostly toward + movement)
"You walked into everything. The room, the darkness, the fire, the water that burns. You don't stop. The question is — are you running toward something, or away from the stillness?"

**2. The Guardian** (mostly away + stillness)
"You build well. Every wall is in its place. But you came here — to this voice, to this dark. Which means some part of you knows: what you're protecting may be what you're hiding from."

**3. The Contradicted** (mixed pattern)
"You moved between holding and letting go, never fully committing to either. That is not indecision. That is breathing. The only honest rhythm."

**4. The Pivot** (switches direction mid-journey)
"Something shifted in you between the third and fourth question. You began one way and ended another. That hinge — that is where you live."

### Full Devolução Design
- Read the SHAPE of 6 choices, not each individually
- 8-12 devoluções total (covering the most common patterns + pivots)
- Each 4-5 sentences, Oracle voice
- Must feel like the Oracle SAW the visitor through their choices
