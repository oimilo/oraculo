**Critical semantic anchor — DO NOT INVERT:**
- Q6B asks: "Resposta — ou outra pergunta?"
- **A = Resposta** (closed reading, "give me the diagnosis") → routes to NORMAL DEVOLUCAO (one of 8 archetypes)
- **B = Outra pergunta** (open form, "leave me with the shape, not the content") → routes to DEVOLUCAO_ESPELHO_SILENCIOSO
- The AR-01 trigger is `q6b === 'B'` because **B is the dignified refusal of closure**. The visitor who chose B gets the open mirror.
- Default on timeout is `A` (closed reading) — ESPELHO_SILENCIOSO requires a CONSCIOUS choice; silence must never accidentally trigger it.

**Important: ESPELHO_SILENCIOSO is ONE key with 6 segments, NOT 6 separate keys.** Research §8.4 flagged this as a literal-vs-consistency interpretation fork in REQUIREMENTS.md. The decision for Phase 33 is: ship ONE key `DEVOLUCAO_ESPELHO_SILENCIOSO` containing an array of 6 SpeechSegment entries. Rationale: (a) matches the packing pattern of the 8 existing archetypes (DEVOLUCAO_SEEKER, DEVOLUCAO_GUARDIAN, etc., each is one key with N segments → one MP3); (b) `scripts/generate-audio-v3.ts` produces ONE MP3 per SCRIPT key; (c) the "6 MP3s" phrasing in REQUIREMENTS.md is a documentation drift from the segment count.
