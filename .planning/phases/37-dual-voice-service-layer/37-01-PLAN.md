|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-37-02 | Information Disclosure | /api/tts route | accept | Voice IDs (ElevenLabs identifiers) are public values, not secrets. The API key (ELEVENLABS_API_KEY) remains server-side only. No PII involved. |
