Available tags: [thoughtful], [whispering], [sad], [determined], [gentle], [serious], [curious], [warm], [commanding], [pause], [long pause]
Rules:
- Max 1 tag per sentence segment
- Tags prepend directly to text: `[tag]Text here`

- pauseAfter in ms for pacing between segments
- Tag density: sparse, 1-2 per section max to avoid voice instability
