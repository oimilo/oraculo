## PARAISO Choices

**Q5 -- The Unanswerable** (cluster4-transformation.md, Scenario 3):
Level: Deep. Core tension: Carrying vs Releasing.
Setup: Darkness thins. A question forms -- not in words, but as pressure in the chest. No answer. May never have one.
A -- Carry the question: burden of not-knowing as companion. Profound tolerance for ambiguity.
B -- Let dissolve: trust that not everything must be carried. Deepest surrender or practiced forgetting.

**Q6 -- End of the Game** (cluster4-transformation.md, Scenario 6):
Level: Profound. FINAL CHOICE -- meta-level.
Setup: Oracle's voice thins. Space grows quiet. Last moment.
A -- Ask for mirror ("I want to hear what you see in me"): openness to being reflected. Hunger for external validation OR genuine curiosity.
B -- Already know ("I already know what I need to know"): trusts own interior. Deepest arrival OR final defense against being truly seen.

## Devolucao Architecture (from RESEARCH-SYNTHESIS.md)

Read the SHAPE of 6 choices across 2 axes:
- Axis 1: Movement vs Stillness
- Axis 2: Toward vs Away

8 Archetypes to write:
1. **SEEKER** -- mostly toward + movement. "You walked into everything."
2. **GUARDIAN** -- mostly away + stillness. "You build well. Every wall is in its place."
3. **CONTRADICTED** -- mixed pattern, no consistent direction. "You moved between holding and letting go."
4. **PIVOT_EARLY** -- starts one way, switches in first 3 choices. "Something shifted early."
5. **PIVOT_LATE** -- starts one way, switches in last 3 choices. "Something shifted between the third and fourth question."
6. **DEPTH_SEEKER** -- consistently chose the "deeper" option. "You went toward every fire."
7. **SURFACE_KEEPER** -- consistently chose the "safer" option. "You protected yourself at every turn."
8. **MIRROR** -- alternating A/B/A/B pattern. "Perfect balance. Never the same direction twice."

Each devolucao: 4-6 sentences. Oracle voice. Must feel like the Oracle SAW the visitor through their choices. Reflects the pattern, does NOT judge.

## Fallback/Timeout Design
- FALLBACK: Rephrase the choice in minimal words, stay within the world. 1 segment each.
- TIMEOUT: Treat silence as a response/choice. 1 segment each. Some use silence as default choice, others acknowledge the pause.

## ElevenLabs v3 Inflection Tags

Rules: max 1 per segment, sparse (2-3 per section), inflection property on SpeechSegment.
